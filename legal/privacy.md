## Privacy Policy

This site is operated by Marqui Group and publishes public engineering notes under the name “Q’s Logs”.

### Analytics
This site uses privacy-friendly analytics (Google Analytics 4) to understand:
- which notes are read
- how the site is used
- how content improves over time

The following safeguards are in place:
- IP addresses are anonymized
- No advertising or remarketing features are enabled
- No personal data is sold or shared
- Data is used solely for aggregate statistics

### Cookies
This site does not use advertising or tracking cookies.
Only essential, privacy-friendly analytics are used.

### Personal data
No personal data is intentionally collected through this site.
There are no forms, accounts, or user profiling mechanisms.
